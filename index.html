Research

  • Robotics Foundation Models: modular robotic cognition systems for task interpretation, perception, state estimation, reasoning, planning, and closed-loop execution.
  • Vision-Language-Action Models: multimodal systems that connect language, visual understanding, and grounded robot behavior.
  • Active Perception: task-aware perception for identifying relevant objects, receptacles, spatial relations, and environment states.
  • Robust Robot Learning: generalization, failure detection, sim-to-real transfer, and long-horizon embodied decision-making.
